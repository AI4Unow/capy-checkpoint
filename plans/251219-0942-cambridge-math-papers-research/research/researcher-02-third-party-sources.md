# Researcher Report: Third-Party Sources for Cambridge Primary Checkpoint Stage 5 Math

## Overview
Stage 5 Mathematics papers are primarily categorized as "Progression Tests" rather than "Checkpoint" exams (which occur at Stage 6). However, third-party sites frequently bundle them under the Checkpoint umbrella.

## 1. Educational Archives (Free/Partial)

### CURSA / IHMC Public Cmaps
- **URL:** [ihmc.us Archive](https://cmapspublic2.ihmc.us/rid=1N9F26462-23V66S6-2ZHN/Cambridge%20Primary%20ProgressionTests_Past%20Papers.pdf)
- **Years:** Primarily 2014 core archive.
- **Content:** Large PDF compilations of Progression Tests across stages.
- **Reliability:** High (Static academic archive), but UI is dated and slow.
- **Cost:** Free.

### CLAD Zimbabwe (Clad Junior Online Tutoring)
- **URL:** [clad.co.zw](https://clad.co.zw)
- **Years:** Access to various years (2014–2025 mentions).
- **Content:** Interactive digital platform for enrolled students. Provides PDF links for Stage 5 Paper 1 & 2.
- **Reliability:** Moderate (Regional online school).
- **Cost:** Free for some links; full access requires enrollment/subscription.

### CIEnotes
- **URL:** [cienotes.com](https://www.cienotes.com)
- **Years:** Comprehensive range (Specimen to recent).
- **Content:** PDF Question Papers (QP) and Mark Schemes (MS).
- **Reliability:** High (Well-known community repository).
- **Cost:** Free.

### Gamatrain
- **URL:** [gamatrain.com](https://gamatrain.com)
- **Years:** Claims 2025 papers available.
- **Content:** Direct PDF downloads for Stage 5.
- **Reliability:** Moderate.
- **Cost:** Free.

## 2. Paid Collections

### Smart Exam Resources / PrimaryCheckpoint.com
- **URL:** [smartexamresources.com](https://www.smartexamresources.com) | [primarycheckpoint.com](https://www.primarycheckpoint.com)
- **Years:** 2014–2020+.
- **Content:** Topical past papers, solved papers (step-by-step), interactive quizzes.
- **Cost:**
  - Topical Stage 5 Math: ~$36 USD (1 year).
  - 6-Month Full Bundle: ~$216 USD.
- **Reliability:** High (Commercial professional service).

### Smart Edu Hub
- **URL:** [smarteduhub.com](https://www.smarteduhub.com)
- **Years:** Similar to Smart Exam Resources.
- **Content:** Solved/unsolved papers, revision notes, mock exams.
- **Cost:** Subscription-based (Paid Members Area).
- **Reliability:** High.

## 3. Format & Quality Summary
- **Format:** Almost exclusively PDF. Paid sites offer "Solved" versions (handwritten or typed explanations).
- **Scan Quality:**
  - Archives (IHMC): Variable, some older scans are blurry.
  - Paid Sites: High-quality, often re-digitized/re-typed.
- **Mark Schemes:** Generally included in all listed sources.
