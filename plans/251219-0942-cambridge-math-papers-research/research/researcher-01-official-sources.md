# Researcher Report: Cambridge Primary Math Stage 5 Past Papers

**Date:** 2025-12-19
**Topic:** Official and Legitimate Sources for Stage 5 Math Papers (2010-2024)

## 1. Key Distinction: Checkpoint vs. Progression Tests
- **Stage 6 (Checkpoint):** External exams marked by Cambridge. Papers widely available 2010–2024.
- **Stage 5 (Progression Tests):** Internal assessments for schools. These are the correct papers for Stage 5.

## 3. Years Available (Stage 5 Progression)
- **Current Curriculum:** 2024, 2023 (New 0–50 scale)
- **Legacy Curriculum:** 2022, 2020, 2018, 2017, 2014
- **Note:** 2023+ papers align with the most recent Cambridge framework updates.

## 4. Paper Structure (Stage 5)
Stage 5 typically features two papers per assessment cycle:

| Feature | Paper 1 (Non-Calculator) | Paper 2 (Calculator) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Duration | 45 Minutes | 45 Minutes |
| Total Marks | 40 Marks | 40 Marks |
| Est. Questions | 25–30 | 25–30 |
| Focus | Mental math, core arithmetic | Application, word problems |

## 5. Question Distribution
- **Number & Arithmetic:** ~50%
- **Geometry & Measure:** ~25%
- **Statistics & Data:** ~15%
- **Thinking & Working Mathematically:** Integrated (~10%)

## 6. Mark Schemes
- Available for most archived years (2014, 2018, 2020, 2022, 2024).
- Use specific codes: **cao** (correct answer only), **ft** (follow through), **isw** (ignore subsequent working).
