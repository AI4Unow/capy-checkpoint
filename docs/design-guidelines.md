# Design Guidelines: Capy-Checkpoint

## Visual Identity
**Aesthetic:** Kawaii / Cottagecore
**Keywords:** Soft, Playful, Friendly, Hand-drawn, Pastel, Immersive

## Typography
- **Primary Heading:** 'Fredoka', sans-serif (Rounded, friendly)
- **Body Text:** 'Nunito', sans-serif (Clear, soft edges)
- **Game Text:** 'Baloo 2', display (Bubbly, high visibility)

## Color Palette
- **Primary Pink:** #FFD6E0 (Soft petal)
- **Sage Green:** #DDE5B6 (Leafy green)
- **Cream Yellow:** #FEFAE0 (Yuzu/Sunlight)
- **Sky Blue:** #A2D2FF (Soft sky)
- **Text Color:** #5E503F (Soft dark brown, better than black for cottagecore)

## UI Components
- **Buttons:** Large, rounded (24px+), with subtle 3D lift/shadow.
- **HUD:**
  - Hearts: Red-pink #FF4D6D, SVG icons.
  - Coins: Yellow #FFD60A, circle with 'C' or coin icon.
- **Math Gates:** Arches with flower/vine decorations, large numbers.

## Character: Capy the Aviator
- Cute Capybara shape.
- Small Yuzu fruit on head.
- Simple flapping animation (simulated).
